The perfect Sunday dinner choice for the whole family

Ingredients

  • Pancetta
  • Spaghetti
  • Egg yolks
  • Parmesan
  • Heavy Cream

Steps

  1. Cook pancetta until lightly browned and crisp, then set aside.
  2. Boil spaghetti, reserve two cups of pasta water, and set aside.
  3. Whisk the eggs in a bowl, then whisk in 1 1/2 cips of the hot pasta water.
  4. Whisk in the cheese and cream.
  5. Add the pasta, pancetta, and chicken to the sauce and toss.
