These easy churro cronuts are deep fried crescent roll doughnuts with a cinnamon sugar topping.

Ingredients

  • 2 8-ounce cans refrigerated crescent dough sheets
  • 4 cups oil, or as needed, for frying
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
  • Chocolate sauce
  1. Remove dough from each can and unroll to a rectangular shape.
  2. Fill a large saucepan halfway with oil.
  3. Combine sugar and cinnamon in a shallow dish, set aside.
  4. Carefully lower each cronut into the hot oil.
  5. Serve with chocolate sauce.
