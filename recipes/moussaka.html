Moussaka

Eggplant dish from Greece

This moussaka tastes great! Layers of sliced eggplant are baked in a ground beef sauce and smothered in a creamy white b√©chamel sauce in this classic Greek dish.

Ingredients

  • 3 Eggplants
  • Salt
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 2 onions

Steps

  1. Lay eggplant slices on towel.
  2. Sprinkle with salt.
  3. Fry eggplant until browned.
  4. Melt 1 tablespoon butter in a large skillet.
  5. Add parsley, fine herbs, cinnamon, and 1/4 teaspoon of nutmeg.
